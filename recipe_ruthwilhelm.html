Super Simple Sweet Potato Casserole Recipe!

Here is a recipe for one of my favorite dishes, sweet potato casserole. I love to eat this sweet side with my family on holidays. I hope you will do the same!

Ingredients:

  • 2 1/2 pounds sweet potatoes, peeled and cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 3/4 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup butter (softened)
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped pecans
  • cooking spray
  • 2 cups miniature marshmallows

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375Â°.
  2. Boil sweet potatoes until very tender.
  3. Put potatoes in a bowl with the brown sugar, butter, salt, and vanilla.
  4. Mash together ingredients.
  5. Coat a 11x7 in baking pan with cooking spray.
  6. Scoop potato mixture into pan. Evenly distribute mixture.
  7. Cover with marshmallows and pecans.
  8. Bake at 375 for 25 minutes or until golden brown.

Enjoy with friends and family!
